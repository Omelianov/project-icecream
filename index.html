How it’s made?

For the preparation of milkshakes, the constituent components are mixed in a mixer vessel. Milk is introduced first, then syrups. Whipping cocktails is carried out for 60 seconds at a stirrer rotation speed of 13,000 rpm. Prepared cocktails are immediately poured into glasses or glasses and released.
